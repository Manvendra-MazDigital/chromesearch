Nat Duca: Everybody wants a nice smooth web app, a nice smooth browsing experience. But actually doing that is a really, really difficult problem. And so what we're going to be doing at Chrome summit is both listening to the kinds of problems that users are having — that's why we want everybody to come — and also showing how to architect your app to be jank free in the first place and also how to use our tools to figure out what's broken when things do go wrong. We know that this is hard, but what we don't know is the kinds of looks that people want to implement and where our tooling falls over.

So when you come to us with a piece of content, we want to look at it and see where the tools work, where the tools don't work, and what kinds of things we should be building into Chrome in the future to make that look even better for you. I'm Nat Duca. I'm one of Chrome's graphics hackers. I look forward to busting jank with you at the summit.
